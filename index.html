My Personal Interests

I love being outside in the nature. Some of the things I really enjoy doing outside are hiking, camping, and fishing. If I was not so busy with school at the moment, I would have done those things more often than I do right now. However, this is one of the reasons I moved to the state of Utah from Florida; the nature is outstanding here.

I also love cooking. Before I moved to the United States from Sweden to pursue a degree in Computer Science, I used to work as a chef at different restaurants in Gothenburg. I really enjoy cooking traditional Swedish food, especially now when I live here in the US. However, I love to try new recipes, and I do not hesitate to cook something I never tried before.

Another thing I love to do is traveling and explore new places. Every chance my boyfriend and I gets to hit the road on a roadtrip for example, we do it. I find it very interesting to see that people, the enviroment, and the food culture differs so much from place to place. Moreover, one of my dream places to travel to is Italy, and hopefully, I get the chance to travel there some day.
